Getting Started with Fae

Fae is your personal AI assistant that listens, thinks, and speaks.

First Launch

1. Grant microphone access when prompted.
2. Fae will download voice models (~200 MB). The orb pulses while loading.
3. When the orb settles to a calm idle state, Fae is ready.

Talking to Fae

Simply speak naturally. The orb glows brighter when Fae is listening.

You can also type messages using the input bar at the bottom of the conversation window.

The Orb

The orb reflects Fae's state:

Idle — calm, gentle pulse. Ready for input.

Listening — brighter glow. Fae hears you.

Thinking — faster animation. Processing your request.

Speaking — rhythmic pulse. Fae is responding.

Settings

Open Cmd+, to configure audio devices, view model status, manage permissions, or re-run onboarding.
